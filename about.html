Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Results

G&W Realty was founded by Sidharth Gaba and Abhinav Wadhawan with a simple belief: every client deserves honest, hands-on service and a team that treats their real estate goals as seriously as their own.

Based in Oshawa and backed by the global resources of Keller Williams Energy Real Estate, we combine local neighbourhood knowledge with world-class marketing tools to deliver results that exceed expectations.

Whether you speak English or Hindi, buying your first home or selling your fifth, we make the process seamless and stress-free.
